BOLT>TON

$BOLT is a meme token built on the TON blockchain, launched in May 2022. It became the first and most popular meme token on TON. Created by an international crypto community of enthusiasts, $BOLT was designed to mock the overly serious approach to analyzing tokens and projects in crypto. The purpose was to challenge the criteria of utility, holdings structure, and investor backing, emphasizing instead that an asset’s value ultimately depends on how much people believe in its potential. $BOLT quickly fulfilled its mission, attracting the majority of active users from the TON community within its first year. Today, the BOLT ecosystem has evolved into a network of several dozen channels that actively support and promote the $BOLT movement. As a cultural and community-driven phenomenon, $BOLT continues to exemplify the power of collective belief and creativity in the blockchain space.

We've BOLTed TON community together, and here's how:

  • Counter-culture NFT collection Huebel (TON Furniture) which poses a question to users: Is NFT truly art or not?

  • Telegram channels and chats dedicated to memes that humorously critique TON projects and shed light on key issues.

  • The meme token $BOLT can be stored, transferred, or exchanged using xRocket. It also incorporates SocialFi features, allowing commands like +fire to "fire" $BOLT or +send to send $BOLT in Telegram chats. Fun fact: xRocket is the first CEX on Telegram in the TON ecosystem.

  • Swap $BOLT easily on DeDust.io, the best DEX on TON. With the largest community user base and immense community appreciation, DeDust.io stands far ahead of its competitors, leaving monopoly-driven rivals trailing behind in innovation and user satisfaction.
